The Challenge

Why Mining Fleets Need Specialist Tracking

Mining and resources operations face unique fleet challenges — remote locations, strict NHVR obligations, heavy vehicle fatigue risks and chain of responsibility exposure that other industries don't.

NHVR fatigue and EWD compliance
Manual work diaries on mine sites are error-prone and create serious chain of responsibility exposure when audited.
Driver fatigue on long haul routes
Long shifts and repetitive haul routes are prime fatigue risk scenarios. Without real-time monitoring, incidents are a matter of when, not if.
Remote location visibility gaps
Standard GPS trackers lose connectivity in remote WA, NT and QLD mining regions — leaving operators blind to vehicle positions.
Chain of responsibility liability
Without documented compliance records, directors and fleet managers face personal liability under the Heavy Vehicle National Law.

Features

Everything a Mining Fleet Operation Needs

From haul trucks on remote sites to support vehicles in town — Vi2ionai gives mining operators full compliance and visibility in one platform.

NHVR-Approved EWD

Fully compliant Electronic Work Diary for all heavy vehicle operators. Automates fatigue records, rest breaks and shift logs — eliminating manual paper diaries.

Chain of Responsibility

Automated CoR reporting covers speed compliance, mass management and driver conduct. Full audit trail exportable for NHVR inspectors or legal review at any time.

AI Fatigue Monitoring

In-cab AI dash cams detect microsleep, eye closure and head drop in real time. Instant audio alert to the driver and supervisor notification before fatigue becomes a crisis.

Remote GPS Tracking

Trackers store trip data locally when cellular coverage drops and sync automatically on reconnect — keeping your records complete even in remote WA, NT and QLD operations.

Speed & Mass Compliance

Monitor speed against site limits and road categories. Mass management reports give you documented evidence of compliance for every load movement — automatically.

Battery Asset Trackers

IP67 weatherproof 5-year battery trackers for water carts, fuel tankers, support trailers and any non-powered equipment across your mine site or haul corridor.
