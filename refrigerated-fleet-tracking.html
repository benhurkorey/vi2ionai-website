The Challenge

Cold Chain Failures Cost Australian Businesses Millions

A single temperature excursion can write off an entire load. Without real-time monitoring, you're relying on drivers to report problems — by which time it's already too late.

Undetected temperature excursions
Refrigeration unit faults, door seals and driver errors go unnoticed until load delivery — too late to save the product.
Manual HACCP record keeping
Paper temperature logs are time-consuming, error-prone and hard to access during an audit or food safety investigation.
No GPS + temperature integration
Running separate GPS and temperature systems means cross-referencing data manually — doubling admin time and creating gaps in your evidence trail.
Regulatory and customer audit risk
FSANZ and customer food safety requirements demand documented cold chain evidence. Missing records can mean lost contracts or regulatory action.
